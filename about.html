Uni Events Hub

All the public events from across different departments and societies at your uni*. Without having to search on Facebook, Guild Website, University Email and University Website, students will be able find all events across these categories:

  • Outdoors & Adventure (e.g. Day trips, Winter Sports)
  • Health & Wellness (e.g. Social Anxiety, Support)
  • Sports & Fitness (e.g. Fitness, Running, Cycling)
  • Learning (e.g. Book Clubs, philosophy, Intellectual Discussion)
  • Food & Drink
  • Language & Culture
  • Movements (Charity, Peace, Sustainability, Community Service)
  • LGBTQ
  • Film, Media & Entertainment
  • Beliefs & Spirituality (e.g. Mind Body Connection)
  • Science, Games and Technology
  • Arts (e.g. Music Photography Writing Dance Crafts)
  • Fashion & Beauty
  • Social (e.g. Pubs and Bars, Travel)
  • Career & Business

*Currently supporting the University of Liverpool, but more universities will be added soon! Watch this space.
